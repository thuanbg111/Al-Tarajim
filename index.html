Al Tarajim

We are a California nonprofit public benefit corporation. Our work is to expand language access for immigrant and refugee communities through translation, interpretation, education, and navigation to social services.

Learn more See programs

What We Do

Language should never be a barrier to essential services.

  • Certified translation for documents and applications
  • On‑site & remote interpretation for clinics, schools, and agencies
  • Workshops that teach rights, responsibilities, and access to care

Translation

Accurate, culturally aware document translation for families and service partners.

Interpretation

Consecutive & simultaneous interpretation—on site or virtual—by trained volunteers and staff.

Education & Navigation

Workshops, helpline, and referrals that help newcomers navigate health, school, and legal systems.
