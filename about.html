About Al Tarajim

Al Tarajim is organized under California’s Nonprofit Public Benefit Corporation Law and operates for public and charitable purposes as a recognized 501(c)(3) (EIN 88-2938721).

Our focus is language access: translation, interpretation, and education that open doors to healthcare, schools, jobs, and justice for limited‑English‑proficient neighbors.

  • No substantial part of our activities involves lobbying or political campaigning.
  • Assets are irrevocably dedicated to charitable and educational purposes.
  • Upon dissolution, remaining assets go to a similar 501(c)(3).
